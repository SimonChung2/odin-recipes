Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

First make the meat sauce. Then cook the noodles and make the ricotta mixture

Layer the lasagna. Cover with foil and bake. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 can tomatoes

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Put ingredients together
  3. Bake in oven for 25mins
